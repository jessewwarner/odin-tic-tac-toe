Tic-Tac-Toe Rules

Tic-Tac-Toe is a two-player game where the players take turns marking a square in a 3x3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game.

How to Play

  1. Player 1 is blue, and Player 2 is red.
  2. Players take turns placing their mark in an empty square.
  3. The game continues until one player wins or the board is full (a draw).
